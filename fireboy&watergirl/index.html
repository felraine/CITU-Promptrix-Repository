Forest Temple — Tethered

Two-player puzzle platformer. You’re chained together—cooperate!

Controls

🔵 Watergirl: A D move, W jump

⏸ Pause: Top-bar button

🔁 Reset: Top-bar button

Goal

• Get 🔴 to the red exit and 🔵 to the blue exit.

• Press plates to open gates.

• Don’t over-stretch the chain.

• Beware the rising murky water.
